The most common questions teachers ask about Rollama:

Spelling, grammar and punctuation are fundamental to clear Standard English, but they can be particularly difficult to learn and teach. Inaccuracies can undermine the overall quality of students' writing, so strengthening these foundations can make a big difference.

It is difficult to create time in the regular curriculum to meet the need for regular, thorough teaching of grammar accuracy.

This is even harder in a mixed class with L1 (first language) and L2 (second language) learners, as many foundational grammar rules are learned innately by L1 speakers from an early age.

Rollama has whole class, multiplayer and solo activities for regular targeted practice.

All our activities are based on recall or rapid application of known patterns using a multiple choice system. This enables tight focus on the grammar, spelling or punctuation objective, and a clear path to reach fluency.

As long or short as you like; we recommend snappy and frequent usage. Frequent practice is needed to enable standard English to roll off the tongue (and from the pen). Repetition is the mother of skill, yet repetition without novelty becomes dull.

Some quiz sites take up to 10 minutes to complete one game; Rollama can be played in 60 seconds.

Rollama is for learners at any age or stage.

We have included aspects of English from first words up to things which adult native speakers sometimes get wrong too.
