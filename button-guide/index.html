What our buttons do

Return to homepage.

Open the teacher tip screen.

Generate a worksheet of 20 random questions, with choice of difficulty level.

Open the points leaderboard.

Select the difficulty level. Definitions are fuzzy, but generally:
- Level 1 KS1 (age 5-7)
- Level 2 KS2 (age 7-11)
- Level 3 KS3 (age 11-14)

Roll to generate a new question within the selected difficulty level.

Show two random student names.

Click on a student's name to award a point.

Show the answer.

Hand signals which the whole class can use to show their answer simultaneously. A quick method of formative assessment to give the teacher immediate evidence of the group's understanding of the focus.

“Ready, steady, show!”

On the homepage, click to copy-paste a whole class list (one name per line)
